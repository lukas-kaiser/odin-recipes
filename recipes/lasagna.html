Lasagna

Description

This traditional, full-flavored lasagna is layered together in just minutes with pasta sauce, noodles, and three cheeses.

Enjoy the taste of one of the classics of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 (26 ounce) jar Prego® Traditional Italian Sauce
  • 6 each uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Â¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Spread about 1 cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish (11x7-inch).
  2. Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese and 1 cup pasta sauce.
  3. Top with remaining 3 uncooked noodles and remaining pasta sauce and cover it.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour.
  5. Uncover and top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  6. Let stand for 5 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
